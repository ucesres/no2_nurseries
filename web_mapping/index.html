Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2) and Schools

This map shows UK educational establishments relative to NO2 concentrations in the air, from background sources and at the roadside.

Use the menu to the right hand side to turn layers on and off and zoom in to see more detail and extra layers. Click on schools to get location specific information.

Schools which are less than 150 m from roads which exceed the European Union legal limit value of 40 µg/m3 have been extracted from national databases of educational establishments.

All schools from those databases are described, showing background concentration and the distance to the nearest road exceeding the limit value.

Defra data describes concentrations 4 m from the kerb, distances are measured to their representation of the road centre, therefore it is likely that distances have been overestimated. School locations may be incorrect, where you think this is the case please use the feedvabck link in the popup and provide a more accurate positional estimate.

For a full list of data sources and a description of the method used to derive distances see the Link text.
